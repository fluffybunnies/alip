Growing up in Portland, Oregon, singer/songwriter Emmanuel Franco, began his musical journey at age 8 after learning to play guitar with his dad. But it wasn’t until 2014, when he was dragged to an Ed Sheeran concert by a friend, that his interest piqued and he was inspired to embark on a musical career. He started singing, quit his job, dropped out of college, broke up with the girlfriend and moved to Hawaii to reinvent himself. Now residing in Venice, California, he is working on releasing his debut EP. Drawing inspiration from the likes of Elvis Presley and Jeff Buckley, he creates a heartfelt sound with pop and rock/country influences, infused with a little bit of the 70s.


“I just want to make people smile… or cry or whatever they feel as long as they feel something and can enjoy the human experience a bit more.”

- Emmanuel Franco
